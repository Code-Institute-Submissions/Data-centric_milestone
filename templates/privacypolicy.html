Privacy Policy

What personal data do we collect?

We will collect and use your personal data (in this case your username, email address and any walks you have submitted to the website). We only collect the essentials required for identifying you on the website, allowing you to create content and contacting you when requested. We do not collect or monitor your viewing habits or favourites. However, we will intervene if sensitive, harmful or dangerous information is posted on this site in your userpage or content added, especially if brought to out attention from other users.

How do we collect your data?

The majority of data we collect is provided upon registration in the form of your username and email address. We also collect data in the form of cookies when you register or log in. Any other information is volunteered to the website for the creation of walks and in the context of comments/issues or compliments sent to our email account and social media accounts.

How will we use your data?

Our company collects your data for two purposes:

  • For the creation and distribution of walks to and from users. This allows for us to provide a platform where likeminded walkers and explorers can share their experiences and interests.
  • To respond to any queries or comments you have provided to us; most often forgotten passwords, incorrect material on created walks or concerns with the websites functionality.
We do not sell or share any of your data with any third parties or companies.

What are your data protection rights?

Every user is entitled to the following:

  • The right to access - You have the right to request us for copies of any personal data you have on our site.
  • The right to erasure - You have the right to request that we delete your personal data, with confirmation and under certain conditions.
  • The right to rectification/completion - You have the right to request that we correct or complete any information which you feel is incorrect or incomplete.
  • The right to access - You have the right to request us for copies of any personal data you have on our site.
  • The right to prevent processing - You have the right to ask that we do not process or restrict processing of any personal data.
If you would like to make a request, we have one month to respond. Requests can be made at our email or contact us.

What are cookies?

Cookies are text files placed on your computer when you visit a website to collect basic internet log information and behaviour. When you visit websites, information can be collected automatically through cookies. We use cookies exclusively to keep you signed in when you log in and to allow access to features such as adding/editing walks and making a list of favourite walking routes.

Can I manage my cookies?

Yes you can, your browser can be set to not accept cookies (though most websites accept cookies by default) and remove them from your browser. More information can be found at https://www.aboutcookies.org.uk/ if you would like to explore these options. However, the features offered by cookies in the above section will no longer be available if you do restrict cookies.

Changes to our Privacy Policy

We will always try to conform to the best practices for safeguarding user information and will inform all registered users by email of any policy changes to our terms of service.

If you have any complaints

If you have any complaints about how we have handled your personal data or information you are welcome to contact us and we will respond to discuss the problems at hand. If you feel we are not addressing your concern correctly then you can contact the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at their website and sent them our email address.
